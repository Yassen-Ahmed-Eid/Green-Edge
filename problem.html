The Problem

Post-Harvest Losses and Storage Challenges in Tanzania

Tanzania faces significant challenges in agriculture, especially regarding the storage of fruits and vegetables. Due to inadequate storage facilities and lack of temperature and humidity control, large portions of harvested produce spoil before reaching the market. These post-harvest losses can reach up to 30-40%, severely impacting farmersâ€™ incomes and food security in the country. Improving storage solutions is critical to reducing waste, enhancing the quality of produce, and supporting sustainable agriculture in Tanzania.

Vision and Mission

At Green Aura, we focus on reducing post-harvest losses in Tanzania by using smart storage technology that controls temperature and humidity. Our mission is to support Tanzanian farmers in preserving fresh produce longer, improving their income and food security. We selected Tanzania for its rich agricultural potential and urgent need for better storage solutions to prevent spoilage and waste in local communities.
