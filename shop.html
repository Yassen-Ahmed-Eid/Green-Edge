Vision and Mission

At Green Aura, we focus on reducing post-harvest losses in Tanzania by using smart storage technology that controls temperature and humidity. Our mission is to support Tanzanian farmers in preserving fresh produce longer, improving their income and food security. We selected Tanzania for its rich agricultural potential and urgent need for better storage solutions to prevent spoilage and waste in local communities.
