About Us

Advanced thermal storage solutions preserving Tanzania fresh produce.

We’re developing an intelligent thermal storage solution in Tanzania designed to preserve the freshness of fruits and vegetables by continuously monitoring and controlling temperature and humidity in real time.
Our system ensures optimal storage conditions to reduce spoilage and extend shelf life, helping farmers and distributors maintain quality throughout the supply chain.
By integrating smart sensors with automated climate control, we empower local producers with technology that keeps their harvests fresh, reduces waste, and increases profitability.

Challenges Facing Agriculture

Post-Harvest Losses Due to Poor Storage in Tanzania

One of the biggest challenges in Tanzania’s agriculture is the significant loss of fruits and vegetables after harvest, primarily due to inadequate storage facilities.
Without proper temperature and humidity control, large quantities of produce spoil quickly, leading to losses estimated at up to 30-40% of total yield.
These losses not only affect farmers' incomes but also contribute to food insecurity and economic instability in rural communities.

Vision and Mission

At Green Aura, we focus on reducing post-harvest losses in Tanzania by using smart storage technology that controls temperature and humidity. Our mission is to support Tanzanian farmers in preserving fresh produce longer, improving their income and food security. We selected Tanzania for its rich agricultural potential and urgent need for better storage solutions to prevent spoilage and waste in local communities.
